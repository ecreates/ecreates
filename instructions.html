Please place a webcam so that your face points directly to it at all times. A camera positioned on top of an additional monitor produces the best results. When ready to be monitored by the system, please press "Start monitoring" button. The system projects your webcam feed on canvas located on "Home" page and begins to analyse it utilising Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.

The analysing process occurs in user's web browser. Therefore, no privacy issues arise from transmitting user images over a network. When the system concludes that there is a 90% or higher probability that a user picks their nose, bites their fingernails, turns the camera away or covers the camera, a continuous loud warning sound is played until a user stops these behaviours. Please be aware that the warning sound is annoying! In addition, user states and their detection accuracy are displayed when a user is monitored.
